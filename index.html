Welcome to Sol Café

Sol Café is where great conversations and unforgettable moments are brewed daily.

Sol Café is a cozy, vibrant coffee shop that offers a warm, inviting atmosphere for coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts alike. Known for its exceptional blends, handcrafted beverages, and friendly resident cats, Sol Café serves everything from rich espressos to refreshing iced coffees. Whether you're looking to unwind with a latte, enjoy the company of our playful cats, or grab a quick espresso on the go, Sol Café is where great conversations and unforgettable moments are brewed daily.
